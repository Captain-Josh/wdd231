PharmaCare Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

PharmaCare Hub

This name reflects a central platform for pharmaceutical knowledge and healthcare awareness. "PharmaCare" highlights medication and care, while "Hub" suggests a place where users can find important health information.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide reliable information about health conditions, drug usage, side effects, and proper medication practices. It will also help users locate nearby pharmacies and healthcare services. The website aims to improve health awareness and promote safe medication practices.

Scenarios

  • What are the uses and side effects of a specific drug?
  • How can I properly manage conditions like diabetes or kidney disease?
  • Where can I find nearby pharmacies or healthcare services?

Color Scheme

  • #ffffff (White): Used for page and section backgrounds.
  • #000000 (Black): Used for main body text.
  • #a4161a (Deep Red): Used for headings and important highlights.
  • #000080 (Navy Blue): Used for navigation, links, and buttons.
  • #FFD700 (Gold): Used for accents and call-to-action elements.
  • #2D6A4F (Green): Used for health-related emphasis sections.
  • #eee (Light Gray): Used for section backgrounds and borders.

Typography

  • Barlow: Used for headings and titles.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs.
